What we do

We only do five things to concrete. We do all of them well.

The crew that sealed your neighbor's deck probably also poured it and hung a gate. We don't pour and we don't frame — we stain, seal, and restore, which is why we know how your slab will take color before we quote it.

Concrete Staining & Decorative Finishes

Driveways, patios, and walkways transformed with rich, variegated color that becomes part of the concrete — not a coating that peels.

Concrete Sealing

Oil, salt air, and sprinkler water don't wash off bare concrete — they soak in and stain it for good. Sealing keeps the surface closed so they never get the chance. The cheapest protection your concrete will get, for one driveway or a whole HOA.

Learn about sealing

Concrete Restoration

Worn, stained, or tired surfaces brought back — cleaned, repaired, refinished. Often for a fraction of the cost of tearing out and repouring.

How it works

Every job runs the same four steps — and prep is the one that decides how long it lasts.

01 / ASSESS

On-site assessment

We evaluate your concrete's condition, moisture, and prior coatings — then recommend only what it actually needs.

02 / PREP

Surface preparation

Cleaning, repair, and profiling. Prep is 70% of a finish that lasts — it's where we refuse to cut corners.

03 / FINISH

Stain & finish

Color and texture applied by hand, sampled and approved with you before we commit to the full surface.

04 / SEAL

Seal & cure

Coastal-grade protection, proper cure time, and a final walkthrough. You sign off — then we photograph the after.
